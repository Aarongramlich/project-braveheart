How does Project Braveheart help?


Braveheart is a premier service to help you take full advantage of the California Consumer Protection Act.


Watch the video on the right to learn more.

HOW IT WORKS

Choose your package.

Choose one of our packages of services and any add-ons
to get started. Don't worry, you can always upgrade later.

View Products

A personal data experts will be assigned to help you through the entire process.

This will be your partner through the process, who will be doing all the heavy lifting. They'll be following up with the companies you've requested data from on a daily basis.

We pride ourselves on keeping an open line of communication, so feel free to reach out to your expert at any time.

Get your data back from the companies gathering it.

According to the CCPA, businesses must provide all applicable data within
45 days of the original request. Once you receive your data from
each company, you'll get to choose what to do with it.

Click here for an example of what you'll receive.

Find out which companies they've sold your data to.

After you receive your data from each company, they are required to tell you which companies they've shared or sold it to. Don't worry, we can help you get your data from those companies too.

Have your data deleted, with guidance from your data expert.

Once you know what information a company is storing on you, you'll be
able to make the judgement call on if you want them to delete your
data, or not. Your data expert will provide advice, and help you through
the process.
