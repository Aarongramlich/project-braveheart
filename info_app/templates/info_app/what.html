What is Project Braveheart?


Braveheart is a premier service to help you take full advantage of the California Consumer Protection Act.


Watch the video on the right to learn more.

WHAT WE DELIVER

Find out what companies know about you.

Companies store a lot of data on you and your family.
We'll find out what categories of data they are storing on you.

Find out what these companies are doing with your data, and what exactly they're using it for.

Whether it's for marketing or sales purposes, companies are analyzing your data to predict your actions and know your habits. We help you find out what these companies are doing with your data so you can gain the knowledge you deserve.

Find out who your data is being sold to.

Companies are constantly buying, selling, and sharing your data with other companies. We'll find out who they've sold your data to, so that we can get
your data back from them as well.

Delete your data from their databases, if you want.

Once you've found out what data these companies have, we'll help you wipe it clean from their databases, if you want.

Automatically opt-out of sharing your data.

Opting out of any future using and sharing of your data is the best way to
ensure privacy. We automatically request each of these companies opt you
out of any future usage and sharing of data.
